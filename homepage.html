INTRODUCTION

The water cycle explains how water moves constantly through the Earth and in the atmosphere. It is an elaborate system with numerous interconnected processes. Water vapor is created when liquid water evaporates, condenses to form clouds, and then precipitates to Earth as rain and snow.
